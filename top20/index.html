What is Top 20 Coders?

The Top 20 Coders competition is a platform for all tech enthusiasts to showcase their coding skills and problem-solving abilities. The competition is designed to challenge participants to solve real-world problems and develop innovative solutions.

Participants will have the opportunity to work on a variety of coding challenges and demonstrate their creativity and technical expertise.
The competition is open to all students of SJCET Palai, and participants will have the chance to win exciting prizes and recognition for their coding skills.

So, if you are passionate about coding and looking for a platform to showcase your talent, then the Top 20 Coders competition is the perfect opportunity for you!
Join us and be a part of this exciting coding competition!

Top 20 Coders Competiton Guidelines

This event is exclusively for SJCET Palai students, primarily targeting students in their 1st, 2nd, and 3rd years.

The first phase of the competition will be an simple introduction and showcase of your proof of work. After this phase students are eligible to participate in second phase.

Second phase required a mulearn and discord accounts to partcipate. Students with programming knowledge can easy win this phase.

Third phase requires strong programming and real world project experience. Each task includes a real world problem statement. And you have to solve and deploy it.

The final phase is a live coding competition and hackathons. The top 20 coders will be selected based on the performance in this phase.

After awarding the top 20 coders, the coders can join the "Pitch my CTO" program. It like shark tank, but you guys are the CTO of the company and founders will pitch their ideas to you.
